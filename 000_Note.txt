 Terminologies:
 ---------------
 - Nodes: Consist of Multiple PODS
 - Cluster: Consist of Multiple NODES
 - PODS: Consist of ONE of Multiple Containers
 - Service: Deployment of Application to face Public
 - Containers: 1 process per container
